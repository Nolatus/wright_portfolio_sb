About me

Jacob “Nolatus” Wright is a GFX designer from Windsor Ontario Canada, With deep roots based in esports culture and sports media. Taking the lessons learned from the virtual world, he wants to create a more interactive and personal experience for fans in traditional sports. He works to employs various types of design and media to help tell a narrative or elevate a brand or company. From motion, static, video, photography. His work strives to inspire the next generation of creators or sports fans helping them to bring their own storytelling perspectives to life.
